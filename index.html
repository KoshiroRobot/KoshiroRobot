Koshiro Robot Creator

I am Koshiro Taisei, a fourth-year student at Hiroshima University. Through robot creation, I have been honing skills in mechanical design, circuit design, control engineering, and more, while exploring the potential of robotics.

I focus on sharing the fascination of robotics with a wide audience by publishing the creation process of my robots on YouTube.

My Journey

  • August 2002: Born in Japan.
  • 2013: Discovered the humanoid robot "Robi" at a bookstore and became fascinated with robotics.
  • April 2018: Enrolled in the Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Program at Tokuyama College of Technology.
  • March 2023: Graduated from Tokuyama College of Technology.
  • April 2023: Transferred to Hiroshima University, studying in the Electrical Systems and Information Engineering Program.
  • February 2024: Launched the YouTube channel "Koshiro Robot Creator".
